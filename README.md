# Logistic_Shipments_Tracking_PowerBI
This dashboard, titled In-Transit Orders, is designed to provide real-time visibility into logistics performance and shipment statuses. It focuses on tracking On-Time Delivery (OTD) metrics and delivery scoring across a variety of customer accounts and geographic routes.

Key Features
1. Estimated Arrival Score Matrix
The left visual is a performance matrix that categorizes active shipments by Customer Name and an Arrival Score (-11, 0, 1).

Performance Heatmap: Uses color-coding (Red/Blue) to immediately highlight deviations in delivery performance.

Customer-Level Insights: Tracks volume for major clients including Noon, Ninja, Lulu, and Danube.

Volume Tracking: Provides a quick aggregate view of the 37 orders currently in the system.

2. Detailed Shipment Log
The right-hand table provides a granular, row-level breakdown of all active shipments, featuring:

Operational Identifiers: Unique Delivery (Del #) and Order numbers for easy cross-referencing.

Route Visibility: Tracks logistics paths such as JDC-RIYADH, MIC-JEDDAH, and MPC2-ABHA.

Timeline Benchmarking: Compares the Actual GI Date (Goods Issue) against the Estimated Arrival Date to monitor transit efficiency.

OTD Status: Automatically flags shipments as "Early" or "Late", allowing for proactive management of potential delays.

Technical Overview
Data Focus: Supply Chain Logistics & Performance Reporting.

Primary Metric: On-Time Delivery (OTD).

Current State: All orders listed are currently in the "In-Transit" status, providing a snapshot of the active delivery pipeline for late March 2026.
